Frequently asked questions

What is Team Horizon?
We're a group of UG students from multiple streams of CUSAT with primary goal to participate in URC (University Rover Challenge) and build Mars Rover.
Can I join the Horizon Team?
If you're a student of CUSAT and have a knack for problem solving and real word implementations in the fields of Electronics, Software Engineering and Mechanical Engineering, please do!
How to join Team Horizon?
You can send us a DM on our LinkedIn page and we'll get to you asap.
Can I join without an engineering background?
Absolutely. There are a lot of position that require managerial and leadership qualities. So even if you don't have an engineering background, you're welcome to apply!
What are the perks of participating?
If we win, we stand a chance to go to Mars Desert Research Station (MDRS) in Utah, USA.
I am a beginner in technology, Can I join Horizon?
We value students from all domains with a wide range of skill sets, ergo, we can definitely find a spot for you if you're interested enough!
